About Diamonds

Diamond is the hardest natural substance on earth. It is known for its brilliance, durability, and purity. Diamonds are widely used in jewelry and are a symbol of trust, strength, and luxury.

Emerald (Panna)

Green gemstone associated with intelligence, communication, and growth. Recommended for Mercury planet.

Ruby (Manik)

Red gemstone symbolizing power and confidence. Associated with the Sun.

Sapphire (Neelam)

Deep blue gemstone known for discipline and protection. Related to Saturn.

Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj)

Yellow gemstone linked with wisdom, wealth, and prosperity. Associated with Jupiter.

Pearl (Moti)

White gemstone known for peace of mind and emotional balance. Related to the Moon.

Coral (Moonga)

Red-orange gemstone representing courage and energy. Associated with Mars.

Opal

Opal is known for creativity, luxury, and attraction. It is associated with the planet Venus and enhances charm.

Hessonite (Gomed)

Honey-colored gemstone used to reduce negative effects of Rahu. Helps in stability and decision-making.
